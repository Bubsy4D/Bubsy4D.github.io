Off-topic: A brief description of my job

Mickael Pot
5nd Dec, 2021
Grenoble, Job activity, software developer
0 Comments

Hello :)

My name is Mickael and I’m a software developper.
This week, I chose to talk to you about what fascinates me, besides travel, my job.
I work for a software development company located in Bernin, in the Isère department, in France.

This company is specialized in the development of software for convenience store.
Today, I'm going to tell you about a mission I was able to carry out in this company and which delighted me.
A customer, located in Lausanne, Switzerland, was interested in trying our products for the opening of his next store in his city. However, our cashiering software was incompatible with Swiss bank terminal devices.
My role was to develop a software that would serve as a platform between our software, the payment terminal, and the payment system of our client's bank.

Purpose and Status

This was my first mission in this company.
As a result, it was complicated to approach it because it's required skills in banking security that I didn't have, in a computer language that I had never practiced and for a delivery before the opening of the store planned for the next January.
Moreover, I didn't know very well the product the company was selling, which quickly put me in an uncomfortable position.

The different steps of the project

The first action taken was to contact the services providing the payment terminals and to contact the customer to find out his requirements for the product.
The contact was mainly by email and was very cordial. I feel more confident now.
The second action was to contract with the suppliers of banking terminals for a development licence for their products.
Once the contract was signed and the rights obtained, I started to develop the software.
This software had to be the fastest because we didn't want our client's customers to wait too long for the payment to be validated.

This part of my mission lasted 3 weeks, followed by an in-depth test by my company's teams. The software had to be tested many times to cover all possible cases.
The three applications we tested allowed us to discover it and even to learn new things about it. I was also in charge of writing documentation for the technician responsible for installing the software on the client's cash registers.

Knowledge required and Knowledge Gained

I had to learn a new programming language, C#.
I was panicked at the thought of designing such software for my first experience with this language. But, to my surprise, the language was very similar to Java, another programming language, which I know quite well.
So, it was easier than the initial situation suggested...

...So here I am, proud to have a new language on my CV.

The next project

This time, I intend to tell you about a future project that we are currently working on with Matteo.
It's a quiz that will allow you to go beyond your own limits but ...hush... it's still a secret project ;)

See you soon.
